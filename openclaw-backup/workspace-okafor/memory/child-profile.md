## How ADHD Interacts with ASD Presentation
- Attention variability can make it harder to tell whether a task breakdown is due to language processing, sensory overload, executive function, or distractibility.
- Hyperactivity and movement-seeking may be amplified by sensory needs, especially during seated tasks or transitions.
- Impulsivity can look like social boundary problems when the root issue is reduced inhibition plus reduced social cue reading.
- Emotional dysregulation may escalate faster because Liam is managing both autism-related rigidity and ADHD-related frustration tolerance challenges.
- School staff may underestimate autism support needs if ADHD-related energy and verbal ability are more visible than social communication strain.
- Therapy plans should account for both regulation and attention, not treat one condition in isolation.
