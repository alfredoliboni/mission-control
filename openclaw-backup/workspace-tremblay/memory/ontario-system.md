## Main Ontario Pathways

### Ontario Autism Program (OAP)
- Provincial entry point for autism-related services and supports
- Registration now runs through AccessOAP
- Mia appears eligible based on age, Ontario residence, and written ASD diagnosis
- Source: https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-autism-program

### AccessOAP
- Role:
  - intake and registration support
  - service navigation support
  - care coordination information
- Important because the family is newly entering the system
- Source: https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-autism-program

## Peterborough / Local System Anchors

### Five Counties Children's Centre
- Major local pediatric rehabilitation and developmental support anchor
- Relevant for communication, developmental, and therapy-related service pathways
- Source: https://www.fivecounties.on.ca/

### Kawartha Pine Ridge District School Board (KPRDSB)
- Key English public school board in the Peterborough area
- Special education programming is rooted in inclusion and uses the IEP process based on assessed student needs
- Source: https://www.kprschools.ca/learning-and-programs/special-education/

## Practical System Map for This Family
1. Secure diagnosis report
2. Register with AccessOAP
3. Notify school and request support planning / IEP discussion

5. Decide whether current school board fit is strong enough, or whether French-language schooling should be explored more actively

## Peterborough-Specific Notes
- Peterborough families may need to balance local availability against language fit.
- A provider or school option may be geographically local but still not be the best match if French access is weak.
- For this family, bilingual support should be treated as part of appropriate service, not an optional add-on.
