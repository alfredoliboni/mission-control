# Mateus Rivera - Ontario System Map

Last Updated: 2026-04-07
Status: Working overview

## Purpose
This file explains the Ontario support system around Mateus with a Peel / Mississauga focus, so the family can see which systems matter now, which ones matter later, and where transition work can get stuck.

## The Main Systems Around Mateus

### 1. School system, immediate priority
This is the most important current system.

Key roles:
- IEP team
- special education staff
- guidance
- Student Success
- experiential learning / co-op staff
- school board transition contacts

Why it matters now:
- Ontario expects transition planning when a student has an IEP and transition needs are identified.
- School is the cleanest route to documented goals, meetings, accommodations, and early work exposure.

What to push for:
- written transition goals
- employment-readiness goals
- community and independence goals
- job shadowing or volunteer exposure
- planning for later co-op / OYAP where appropriate

Sources:
- https://www.ontario.ca/page/special-education-ontario-policy-and-resource-guide-kindergarten-grade-12
- Peel board site to verify locally: https://www.pdsb.net/
- Dufferin-Peel Catholic special education: https://www.dpcdsb.org/programs-services/special-education

### 2. Children’s treatment / therapy system
Main local anchor:
- ErinoakKids

Why it matters:
- can support service navigation, developmental disability care, therapy, and family-centred planning in Peel
- may help connect clinical goals to independence and transition needs

Best use:
- clarify whether adolescent OT, social work, or service-navigation support can help with transition-to-adulthood planning

Source:
- https://www.erinoakkids.ca/

### 3. Employment system
Main provincial anchor:
- Employment Ontario

Why it matters:
- later-teen path to local employment counselling, youth job supports, skills training referrals, apprenticeship information, and employment service connections

Best use:
- not necessarily the first system to lead at age 14, but important to map early so the family knows where youth employment coaching may come from later

Source:
- https://www.ontario.ca/page/employment-ontario

### 4. Skilled trades / apprenticeship exploration system
Main provincial anchor:
- OYAP

Why it matters:
- useful later if Mateus shows strong fit for structured, hands-on, supervised trade environments
- not an immediate step at his current age and stage, but worth understanding in advance

Source:
- https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-youth-apprenticeship-program

### 5. Adult disability income / benefits system
Main provincial anchor:
- ODSP

Why it matters:
- future adult financial support and health benefits may matter a lot if disability-related barriers remain significant
- the family needs time to organize documents and understand rules before adulthood

Source:
- https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-disability-support-program

## Peel / Mississauga Flow, practical version

### Right now
- school system leads
- family gathers documents
- therapy supports are used to strengthen function and independence
- early work exposure is explored carefully

### Next stage
- school plus employment system overlap
- vocational assessment or work-readiness support becomes more important
- transportation and community independence become more urgent

### Later teen / transition-to-adulthood stage
- employment system grows in importance
- ODSP and disability-benefit planning become more active
- adult-service handoff becomes a bigger issue

## Where Families Commonly Get Stuck
- waiting too long to push the school for explicit transition goals
- assuming diagnosis alone is enough for future benefits
- not keeping copies of assessments and school records
- choosing first work experiences that are too socially or sensory demanding
- treating systems as separate when they need to be coordinated

## Local Strategy For Mateus

### Best current lead system
School.
Reason: it is already active, already documented, and can connect directly to transition planning, work exposure, and accommodations.
- Source: Ontario transition-planning guidance above

### Best secondary support system
ErinoakKids or other youth therapy/service-navigation supports.
Reason: this can help translate autism + ADHD needs into practical supports for independence, regulation, and real-world readiness.
- Source: https://www.erinoakkids.ca/

### Best later bridge system
Employment Ontario.
Reason: it is the most likely provincial route into local employment coaching and youth job support once direct job preparation becomes timely.
- Source: https://www.ontario.ca/page/employment-ontario
