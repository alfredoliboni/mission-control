## Core Documents To Track

### 1. IEP
- Status: Exists, per USER.md
- Need to confirm:

  - current accommodations
  - whether a formal transition plan is included

### 2. Vocational assessment

### 3. Psychological / psychoeducational assessment
- Status: Unknown

- Why it matters: often central for school advocacy, benefit planning, and later adult-service documentation.
- Source: planning recommendation for disability documentation readiness

### 4. School reports
- Status: Unknown collection status
- Need to gather:
  - recent report cards
  - learning skills comments
  - attendance patterns if relevant
  - co-op / experiential learning notes when available
  - guidance or Student Success notes if any exist
- Why it matters: school records can help show strengths, support needs, and transition progress over time.
- Source: planning recommendation tied to school transition pathway

### 5. Therapy and medical records
- Status: Partial, ABA history known; medication review need noted in USER.md
- Need to gather:
  - therapy summaries
  - discharge or reduction notes from ABA if available
  - medication review notes
  - OT, psychology, psychiatry, or speech-language records if any exist
- Why it matters: these records can support transition planning, functional documentation, and future applications.
- Source: planning recommendation tied to ODSP and service-transition preparation, https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-disability-support-program
