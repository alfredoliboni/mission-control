## Detailed Eligibility

### Disability Tax Credit (DTC)
- **Eligibility:** ✅ Likely qualifies (ASD Level 2)
- **Amount:** ~$2,500-3,000/year tax credit
- **Unlocks:** CDB ($3,173/yr), RDSP
- **How Applied:** T2201 form signed by Dr. Patel, mailed Jan 20
- **Expected Response:** 6-8 weeks (check after March 20)
- **⚠️ Action:** Call CRA if no response by April 15 — 1-800-959-8281
- **Documents Needed:** T2201 (sent), diagnosis report (sent)
- **Renewal:** Annual — must re-file if condition changes

### Ontario Autism Program (OAP)
- **Eligibility:** ✅ Enrolled — ASD diagnosis required
- **Amount:** $20,000-$55,000/year based on needs determination
- **How Applied:** Registered through AccessOAP portal on Dec 1
- **Status:** Waiting for Childhood Budget allocation (est. 4-6 months)
- **Foundational Family Services:** Available now — free workshops
- **⚠️ Action:** Attend Entry to OAP workshop (booked April 22)
- **Documents Needed:** Diagnosis report (submitted)
- **Renewal:** Annual review based on needs assessment

### Child Disability Benefit (CDB)
- **Eligibility:** Requires DTC approval — auto-enrolls via CRA
- **Amount:** Up to $248.75/month ($2,985/year) tax-free
- **How Applied:** Automatically applied when DTC approved
- **Expected Response:** Within 8 weeks of DTC approval
- **Documents Needed:** None — auto-linked to DTC
- **Renewal:** Auto-renews annually with tax filing

### Assistance for Children with Severe Disabilities (ACSD)
- **Eligibility:** ⚠️ Needs assessment — child must have severe disability
- **Amount:** Up to $500/month for extraordinary costs
- **How Applied:** Apply through local MCSS office
- **Documents Needed:** Medical documentation, cost receipts
- **⚠️ Action:** Contact MCSS London office to request application — 519-438-5111
- **Renewal:** Annual review

### Special Services at Home (SSAH)
- **Eligibility:** ⚠️ Waitlisted program — high demand
- **Amount:** Up to $4,200/year for respite and support workers
- **How Applied:** Apply through Developmental Services Ontario (DSO)
- **Documents Needed:** Diagnosis report, needs assessment
- **Status:** Provincial waitlist — may take 12+ months
- **Renewal:** Annual review

### RDSP (Registered Disability Savings Plan)
- **Eligibility:** Requires DTC approval
- **Amount:** Government adds up to $3,500/year in grants + $1,000/year in bonds
- **Lifetime Limit:** $200,000
- **Best Move:** Open ASAP after DTC, even with $0 — bonds are income-tested
- **Where:** Any major bank, credit union, or online (BMO, TD, RBC all offer)
- **⚠️ Action:** Open account immediately after DTC approval
