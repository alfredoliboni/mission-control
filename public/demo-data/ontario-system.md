# Ontario Autism System — How It Works (as of 2026)

## The Journey Overview
```
Concerns → Pediatrician → Referral → Assessment → Diagnosis
    → OAP Registration → Needs Determination → Funding → Services
```

## Ontario Autism Program (OAP)

### Entry Points
1. Register at accessoap.ca after diagnosis
2. Needs Determination interview (phone, 2-3 hours)
3. Core Clinical Services OR Childhood Budget allocated

### Childhood Budget
- Based on needs determination (not diagnosis level)
- Range: ~$20,000 — $55,000/year
- Used for: ABA/IBI, OT, SLP, other approved therapies
- Families choose their own service providers
- Must use registered OAP providers

### Foundational Family Services (available immediately)
- Entry to OAP workshops
- Caregiver-mediated coaching
- Online learning modules
- No waitlist for these

### Current Wait Times (London region, est.)
| Service | Wait |
|---------|------|
| OAP Childhood Budget | 4-6 months after registration |
| TVCC OT Assessment | 6-10 months |
| TVCC SLP Assessment | 4-6 months |
| ABA/IBI (private, with funding) | 2-4 months |
| Developmental Pediatrician | 12-18 months (new referral) |

## School System

### Individual Education Plan (IEP)
- Required for students with identified exceptionalities
- IPRC (Identification, Placement, Review Committee) process
- Review: at least annually, parents can request anytime
- EA allocation: determined by school board, not guaranteed

### School Boards in London
- **TVDSB** (Thames Valley District School Board) — public
- **LDCSB** (London District Catholic School Board) — Catholic
- Both have autism specialist teachers and ASD consultants

## Financial Supports
| Benefit | Amount | Eligibility |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| Disability Tax Credit (DTC) | ~$2,500-3,000/year tax reduction | ASD diagnosis, CRA approval |
| Child Disability Benefit (CDB) | Up to $3,173/year | DTC-eligible + income-tested |
| RDSP (savings) | $200k lifetime, grants up to $3,500/yr | DTC-eligible, under 49 |
| Assistance for Children with Severe Disabilities (ACSD) | Up to $500/month | Severe needs, income-tested |
| Special Services at Home (SSAH) | Varies | Respite/personal support |
